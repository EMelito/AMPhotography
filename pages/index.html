Amulree Milne

is a self taught photographer, living in the heart of Toronto. Her passion for capturing the world around her began at a young age and developed from wanting to show others the beauty that lies everywhere. She has a keen eye for life’s special moments and takes great pride in being able to capture them.

“Seeing someone’s face light up when viewing a photo I’ve taken is so validating, it reminds me why I chose this profession and it never gets old.”

-Amulree

Amulree has a unique style that stems from capturing organic scenes. Whether it’s people or objects she prefers to photograph with as little interference as possible. She currently works as an event photographer but enjoys stepping outside of her comfort zone. She believes the most rewarding thing about being a photographer is being able to find ways to continue learning and challenge yourself.

Feel free to contact her with your photography needs or any questions you may have by e-mail and she will respond in a timely manner. She is always happy to discuss photography in any capacity, so don’t be shy.
